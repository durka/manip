intersectBoxes

PURPOSE

INTERSECTBOXES Intersection of two bounding boxes

SYNOPSIS

function box = intersectBoxes(box1, box2)

DESCRIPTION 

INTERSECTBOXES Intersection of two bounding boxes

   RES = intersectBoxes(BOX1, BOX2)

   Example
   box1 = [5 20 5 30];
   box2 = [0 15 0 15];
   intersectBoxes(box1, box2)
   ans = 
       5 15 5 15

   See also
   boxes2d, drawBox, mergeBoxes
