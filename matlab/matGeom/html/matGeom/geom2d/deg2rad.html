deg2rad

PURPOSE

DEG2RAD Convert angle from degrees to radians

SYNOPSIS

function rad = deg2rad(deg)

DESCRIPTION 

DEG2RAD Convert angle from degrees to radians

   Usage:
   R = deg2rad(D)
   convert an angle in degrees to an angle in radians.

   Example
   deg2rad(180)    % gives pi
   ans = 
       3.1416
   deg2rad(60)     % gives pi/3
   ans =
       1.0472

   See Also
   angles2d, rad2deg
